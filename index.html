Plastic Pollution is Destroying Our Planet!

🎀 My Topic

Plastic pollution and its impact on ecosystems and human health.

💭 Why I Chose This

Plastic pollution is one of the most visible environmental issues today. It harms animals, pollutes oceans, and affects human health. I chose this topic because it shows how everyday actions can harm the planet.

📊 Facts

  • 8 million tons of plastic enter oceans yearly
  • Plastic takes 400+ years to break down
  • 100,000 marine animals die yearly

Animals are suffering because of plastic waste.

Oceans are filled with plastic waste that never disappears.

Even beaches are being taken over by plastic pollution.
